tfoot

tfoot 标签用于组合 HTML 表格的页脚内容。

语法：

<table>
    <thead>
        <tr>
            <th>Month</th>
            <th>Savings</th>
        </tr>
    </thead>
    <tbody>
        <tr>
            <td>January</td>
            <td>$100</td>
        </tr>
        <tr>
            <td>February</td>
            <td>$80</td>
        </tr>
    </tbody>
    <tfoot>
        <tr>
            <td>Sum</td>
            <td>$180</td>
        </tr>
    </tfoot>
</table>

效果：

Month Savings
January $100
February $80
Sum $180
