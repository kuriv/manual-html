# q

> q 标签包含的文本是引用，它是一个行内元素，在浏览器中显示为使用引号包含的内容。

语法：

```html
<p>
    WWF's goal is to:
    <q>Build a future where people live in harmony with nature.</q>
    We hope they succeed.
</p>
```

效果：

<p>
    WWF's goal is to:
    <q>Build a future where people live in harmony with nature.</q>
    We hope they succeed.
</p>
